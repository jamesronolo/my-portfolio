About Us

Welcome to James Ronolo Restaurant, a cozy and family-friendly haven located in the heart of Anibongan, Duero, Bohol. We celebrate the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines with every dish we serve. Using fresh, local ingredients and time-honored recipes passed down through generations, we create authentic Filipino cuisine that warms both body and soul.

Whether you're enjoying a hearty breakfast, a satisfying lunch, or a delightful dinner with family and friends, our restaurant offers a welcoming atmosphere where tradition meets hospitality. Come experience the true taste of home.
